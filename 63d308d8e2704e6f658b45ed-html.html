Dak Prescott's new 'rebound girl' is a Tiger: LSU athlete and swimmer Jadyn Jannasch

January 26, 2024
blog

After Dallas Cowboys quarterback Dak Prescott's breakup with girlfriend Natalie Buffett was made public, he now appears to have found a new partner in an athlete who is quite the tiger: LSU swimmer Jadyn Jannasch.

According to Page Six, Prescott and Buffett ended their relationship of more than two years in March of last year, but never made public the fact or the reasons. And it is the same site that claims that Dak's new girl is Jadyn.

She is still studying at Louisiana State

According to Page Six, sources close to the couple claim that they get along well and have been dating occasionally, nothing apparently very serious, as Jadyn, 20, is still studying at LSU and Prescott is in Texas, but the two saw each other during the vacations.

"She's been telling teammates that it's a rebound relationship for him, and it's still going on," according to one insider, "but since she's in school and he's in Texas, it's not an everyday thing."

Jadyn is a Dallas native, and according to the insider took several of her teammates to "a couple of games during the season" to watch Prescott play.

They met through her father

While there is not much information on the matter, it is known that Dak and Jadyn met because her dad, Jeff Jannasch, owns a mortgage company in Texas that partnered with the Cowboys.

In fact, Jeff posted on his social media in 2021 a photo of him with Dak, whom he called "his best friend."

Jadyn loves swimming and chose to attend Louisiana State University so she could be on the same team as her brother, Jack Jannasch, as posted on LSU's page.

Dak was dating Natalie Buffett for two years, who since they broke up in March 2022 has deleted photos she had with the Cowboys QB from her Instagram.

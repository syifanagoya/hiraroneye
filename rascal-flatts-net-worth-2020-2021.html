They stated that the band could disband following their farewell tour after twenty-one years in the business.

Inception

Rascal Flatts was formed in 1999 and is comprised of three members. The members are Gary LeVox (lead vocalist), his second cousin Jay DeMarcus (bass guitar), and Joe Don Rooney (guitar and banjo). Jay DeMarcus was also the brother-in-law of country music singer James Otto and formerly one-half of the Christian music duo, East to West. The group debuted in early 2000 with their hit single, “Prayin’ for Daylight.”

The song reached number three on the Billboard Country charts and was issued on Lyric Street, and after their first single hit, the album’s other three singles made the top ten on the chart. The band then released its second album in 2002, which they co-produced. In this album, it’s first single, “These Days,” became the band’s first number one hit on the U.S. country charts. Apart from that single, the others also did very well on the charts.

The band then released their third album in 2004 called, “Feels Like Today” and its album title track was released as the first single. The album became another success that just continued to prove how good the band actually was. The band released their fourth album in 2006 called, “Me and My Gang.”

The album’s first single release was their own version of ”What Hurts The Most,” which was originally recorded by Mark Wills in 2003. The band still continued to release hit singles under the album such as “My Wish” and “Stand,” which were the third and fourth releases in the album, which both reached number one on the charts.

“Me and My Gang” was the highest US debut in 2006, with 722,000 units in April. It was also the second best-selling album after High School Musical. In 2008, the band released their first compilation album, “Greatest Hits Volume 1” which comprised of thirteen of their biggest singles.

In October 2009, they released a second compilation of their greatest hits. At this moment, the band had received a lot of adoration and respect from listeners in the United States of America. They began performing live shows on big platforms such as the CMT Music Awards, Oprah’s Kickoff Party, and the finale of America’s Got Talent.

Evolution

The band had had an incredible run into their music career, but in April of 2010, Lyric Street was closed. But this did not leave the band stranded, in July of that year, they signed to Big Machine Records. This should’ve been obvious considering that the band had been extremely successful, and many record labels would not want to miss an opportunity to work with them.  Under the new record label, the band was still releasing hit singles that were on the billboard charts. Certainly, even after losing their first record label, their light still shone brightly.

Awards & Achievements

Rascal Flatts has been one of the best country music groups of all time. The band has released singles that have always been topping the Billboard charts. The band is undeniable one of a kind. Because of their awesomeness, the band as been honored in so many ways, one big way is showering them with awards that they deserve such as:

  • People’s Choice Award for favorite Group in 2008 and 2009.
  • Academy of Country Music Award for Song of the Year in 2003.
  • Academy of Country Music Award for Vocal Group of the Year in 2007, 2008 and 2009.
  • Country Music Association Award for Vocal Group of the Year in 2006, 2007 and 2008.

Rick John Roufus (born June 3, 1966) is an American kickboxer who competes out of Phoenix, Arizona’s The MMA Lab. He is one of America’s most well-known kickboxers, and he has also participated in boxing and mixed martial arts. He was a three-time world champion in the weight classes of super middleweight, light heavyweight, and heavyweight. Roufus has held championships in all of the main kickboxing organisations (PKA, IKF, ISKA, FFKA, and KICK), and is noted for his strong kicks and solid boxing abilities. Duke Roufus, who is also a kickboxer, muay Thai and mixed martial arts coach, is his elder brother.
